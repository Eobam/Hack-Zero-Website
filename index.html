What's a Flipper Zero?

A Flipper Zero is a palm-sized multi-tool device for pentesters, developers, and computer enthusiasts. It has a wide variety of features, including a 1 GHZ transciever, RFID and NFC capabilities, Bluetooth features, hardware debugging, a cute dolphin and much more. What we really care about, is the GPIO pins, and the ability to create apps. The Flipper Zero allows us to add extra sensors, and applications with the existing one easily.
